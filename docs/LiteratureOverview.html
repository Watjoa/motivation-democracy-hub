Literature overview

Conceptual backbone

Conceptual framework

The hub integrates Self-Determination Theory (autonomy, competence, relatedness) with political psychology to explain how societal contexts (democratic quality, threat, policy communication) shape psychological needs, which in turn predict well-being and civic attitudes.


Key domains

DOMAIN 1

Basic psychological needs & well-being

Establishes how autonomy, competence, and relatedness predict well-being across contexts and cultures.

DOMAIN 2

Governance & motivation

Links democratic quality and policy environments to need-supportive experiences and engagement.


DOMAIN 3

Threat & Attitudes

Explores how threat and communication styles shift motivation and democratic support.

DOMAIN 4

Cross-cultural measurement & methods

Provides invariance testing, harmonization, and open pipelines for reproducibility.
